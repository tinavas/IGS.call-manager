IGS ENERGY TPV Callflow

 

D2D Agent Portion

Hello this is [verifier name] with IGS verification. May I have the:

a)      Zip code please?

b)     And your Agent ID?

c)      Is this for Electricity or Gas (or both)?

d)     Residential or Commercial?

e)      Verify rate plan with the agent:  You are enrolling the customer for [type of rate plan and price]  right? IF it’s a Dual enrollment, verify the rate plan and price for the other account

 

Gather required info as shown on AE Online (Account#, Service Address, Mailing Address if different, Account Name, Email if provided, Phone Number)

 

And who will I be speaking with?

 

Okay, agent, please tell the customer that I’ll be calling him/her back in 30 seconds. Thank you.

 

For PA Calls:

Okay, agent, I’ll be calling the customer back in 30 seconds. Please remember you will need to leave the premises when the customer is completing their TPV portion. Thank you.

 

D2D Callback Portion

Hello Mr/Ms. ___, this is ___ from IGS Energy, I will now verify your enrollment with IGS Energy as your [natural gas/electricity] supplier in the [utility name] [natural gas/electricity] choice program.

 

Proceed with the verification script…

 

Courtesy Close:

 Okay, your verification is now complete. Thank you for choosing IGS Energy; if we can assist you in the future, please give us a call. Could you hand the phone to the agent so that I can give them a confirmation number for your enrollment with IGS Energy?

 

After the phone is handed to the agent… Hello agent. Here’s your confirmation number. Provide the confirmation number to the agent then say goodbye.

 

Courtesy Close for PA Calls:

Okay, your verification is now complete. Please take down your confirmation number. Provide the confirmation number to the customer.

 

Thank you for choosing IGS Energy; if we can assist you in the future, please give us a call.
